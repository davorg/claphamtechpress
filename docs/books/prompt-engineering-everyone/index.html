About Will Sowman

Will Sowman is an author based in south London. He lives with his wife and their two spirited cats, Monty and Python. A passionate writer with a keen interest in technology and its intersection with our daily lives, Will has always been fascinated by the vast potential of artificial intelligence and its transformative power.
