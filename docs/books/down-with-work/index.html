Down With Work

From Cubicle to Coffee Shop: Redefining Work in the Modern Age

By Will Sowman

Down With Work is a practical guide to rethinking your relationship with work and building a career with more freedom, flexibility and purpose. It covers remote and hybrid roles, negotiating better working arrangements, freelancing, side hustles and other routes toward a professional life on your own terms.

About Will Sowman

Will Sowman is an author based in south London. He lives with his wife and their two spirited cats, Monty and Python. A passionate writer with a keen interest in technology and its intersection with our daily lives, Will has always been fascinated by the vast potential of artificial intelligence and its transformative power.
